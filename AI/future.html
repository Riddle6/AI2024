What is to come?

About 50-55% of company organizations have used AI in some sort of way, especially for business automation. With the rise of digital bots and assistances, businesses can rely on AI to help customers so employees don't have to worry as much.

However, this has led to the fear over job losses due to AI's rapid growth. Employees feel that AI could do a third of the tasks that they perform.

This is not the only problem that has been concerned with AI. With security comes along Data Privacy, but also the fear that your personal information could be exposed to the public.
