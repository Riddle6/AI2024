Early Stages of AI

AI has been a long time with us since the 1950s. The first successful computer AI program back in 1997 was able to beat a Chess Grandmaster player. In 2011, AI won a Jeopardy game!

Since 1997, AI technology has expanded massively, with OpenAI developing and releasing its newest GPT models in 2018. Later, OpenAI also developed ChatGPT since the popularity of AI.
